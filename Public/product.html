Low Profile

The Armadillo lives on your headphones, so not only do you not have to worry about losing it, but it doesn't take up extra space in your pocket. When in use its just a few small pieces attached to the bottom of your headphones, and when wrapped up a small convenient to carry curl.

Fast

We save you time by preventing your headphones from tangling, but we also make your headphones really fast to unwrap. Just simply put your finger in the center ring, grab your earphones, pull and your headphones are ready to use.

Effective

The Armadillo works, while some product are only partially effective at keeping your head phones untangled our product works. By simply wrapping your headphones around our product your headphones will stay tangle free!
